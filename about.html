Learn About Our Business!

We are a technology-driven company centered around building Bangladesh’s largest network of last-mile retail point of sale to deliver our services to the unserved and the underserved rural, semi-urban and urban population of Bangladesh. Our aim is to potentially enable every Bangladeshi to benefit from financial inclusion, social inclusion by pioneering the concept of White Label ATM and White Label POS service in Bangladesh. We want to bring ATM and POS services to the doorsteps of our customers by providing access to ATM in the most rural areas of Bangladesh.
