EMV Contact and Contactless products

Two main forces currently drive the issuance of payment cards worldwide, namely the migration to the EMV interoperability and security standard, and the growing adoption of contactless payments. Both are answers from international and domestic payment schemes and regulators worldwide to address consumers’ natural needs for incremental security, functionality and convenience for their everyday payments.

everyday payments. LENDEN is the authorized reseller of Idemia in Bangladesh. IDEMIA’s global EMV product range features contact and contactless chip-based products, made of secured Java-based open platform operating systems and applications, and certified against industry state-of-the art security and functional requirements. IDEMIA delivers payment cards and services to the world’s largest financial institutions and FinTechs, so that they can provide consumers with debit, ATM, credit or prepaid cards that are certified by all international and domestic schemes worldwide. Depending on the issuer’s needs, our EMV cards can be delivered with added-value applications, as well as innovative card body features and materials.

EMV Contact

Contact EMV refers to the EMV contact chip specifications defining how financial transactions are conducted using contact chip cards. These cards support cryptographic functions to prevent counterfeiting of cards and additional functions that make them more secure than traditional magnetic-stripe cards.
