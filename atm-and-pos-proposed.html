ATM AND POS (Proposed)

Lenden aims to be the pioneer in introducing the concept of White Label ATM and White Label Merchant Acquisition (POS) in Bangladesh. We have applied for the PSO license from Bangladesh Bank to set up and manage White label ATMs, enabling real-time cash withdrawals. Through our vast network of Lenden Centers, we aim to provide every citizen with the facility of access to ATM in the most rural areas of Bangladesh. Our purpose is to provide a reliable network of ATMs that helps the population to undertake the basic financial and non-financial transactions. We aim for greater financial inclusion and intend to include every Bangladeshi under the digital financial ecosystem

Connected to National Payment Switch Bangladesh (NPSB).

Proposed Features

Supports transaction from all Banks cards via NPSB

Financial Transactions facilitating Cash Withdrawal easily and handily

Non-Financial Transactions enabling Balance Enquiry, PIN Change, and Mini Statement

Making financial payment easy and accessible via POS network.

Other value added services through our Lenden centers

We focus on the needs of small to middle market businesses to improve and grow their return.
